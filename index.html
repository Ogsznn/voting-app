Why Blockchain for Voting?

Revolutionizing Nigeria's Electoral System

Blockchain technology ensures a secure, transparent, and tamper-proof voting process. By leveraging a decentralized, immutable ledger, every vote is permanently recorded, eliminating risks of fraud or manipulation.

Built on chain, our platform delivers unmatched scalability, processing millions of votes in real-time with lightning-fast confirmation and minimal costs, making secure and efficient elections a reality for Nigeria.

How It Works?

A simple and secure process in 4 steps

1

Registration and Verification

Create your account on the Lotena platform the necessary information, Our system then verifies your eligibility for the election.

2

Secure Authentication

Before the election, each verified voter will receive a unique, non-transferable token that will be used for the election.

3

Anonymous Voting

Vote for your desired candidate on our intuitive interface. Your vote is encrypted and recorded on the blockchain, ensuring your anonymity while preserving vote integrity.

4

Live Results

Follow the count in real-time on our dashboard. Every vote is immediately tallied and publicly visible, with no possibility of alteration.
