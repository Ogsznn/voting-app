Why Blockchain?

The technology behind a transparent electoral revolution

Blockchain is the ideal technology to modernize Nigeria's electoral system. Using a distributed and immutable ledger, we ensure every vote is permanently and transparently recorded, with no possibility of fraud or manipulation.

Our platform built on Solana offers exceptional scalability, capable of handling millions of votes simultaneously with minimal fees and near-instant confirmation.

How It Works?

A simple and secure process in 4 steps

1

Registration and Verification

Create your account on the LOTENA platform and complete your profile with the necessary documents. Our system verifies your eligibility in real-time using the blockchain.

2

Secure Authentication

During each election, authenticate via our biometric system or your blockchain wallet. You receive a unique, non-transferable voting token.

3

Anonymous Voting

Select your candidates on our intuitive interface. Your vote is encrypted and recorded on the Solana blockchain, ensuring your anonymity while preserving vote integrity.

4

Live Results

Follow the count in real-time on our dashboard. Every vote is immediately tallied and publicly visible, with no possibility of alteration.
